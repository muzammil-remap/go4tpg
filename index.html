Questions, answered

How quickly will we see results?

Your first standard AI workflows are installed within days of a Planning Summit. In a Revenue Operations Sprint, systems go live across the 10 business days... the deeper impact on margin and pipeline shows in the 30 to 90 days that follow.

Do we need to be technical or AI-savvy?

No. We design role-based workflows your people use daily... not training they forget. Adoption support is built into every engagement.

What if we already run EOS or work with an implementer?

The Lighthouse Leadership OS is a direct alternative to EOS, Petra, and Bloom Growth. It delivers the plan, alignment, and accountability those programs sell... plus working AI systems built by our own engineering team, which none of them offer.

What is standard and what is custom?

Standard installations come from TPG’s catalog of proven workflows... lead follow-up, pipeline, proposals, reporting, collections... and are included in each offer by count. Custom development and integrations are always scoped independently, in writing, before we build.

Should we hire an Integrator first?

Usually not first. The Integrator is a real role... the Harvard Business Review named it in 1967... but a true Integrator owns the P&L, breaks ties, and leads the leadership team, and that is a rare, expensive hire. Install the operating system first; much of what an Integrator forces daily... alignment, cadence, follow-through, reporting... is carried by the system and its AI workflows. If you still need the capacity, our Fractional Chief Revenue Architect is the revenue-side Integrator in the original sense... without the cost or risk of the full-time hire.
