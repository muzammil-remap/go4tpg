About / Why TPG

35 years of building what others theorize about.

Enterprise-caliber thinking applied to founder-led businesses that need it most.

Alan Peterson

Operator. Coach. Installer.

Alan Peterson founded The Peterson Group in 1991. For thirty-five years he has built the sales, marketing, and digital infrastructure behind growth companies... from startups to Fortune-level enterprises: Waste Management, Xerox Corporate, Southern California Edison, the County of Los Angeles.

Most executive coaches and system implementers pick one lane. The coach facilitates and listens but has rarely built, scaled, or sold a company. The systems implementer installs a framework but lacks the leadership and personal development depth to make it stick with the humans running it. TPG occupies the harder middle: decades of operating experience, real coaching and facilitation depth, and the AI installation capability that turns a good conversation into a working system by Monday morning. This is not three separate claims. It is one practice... install the system, and install the judgment in the people who have to run it.

That philosophy is the subject of Alan’s forthcoming book, Lifeguard and the Lighthouse. A lifeguard rescues: jumping in, taking over, creating dependency on the rescuer. A lighthouse stands steady and lets the ship navigate itself by its light. Every TPG engagement aims to be the lighthouse.

Alan works globally... from Newport Beach, from Paris, and aboard Cyrolia, his bluewater cruising sailboat, in French Polynesia.

The TPG Philosophy

Five statements. No fine print.

  • Facilitation-led... we extract, we do not lecture.
  • Blueprints, not decks.
  • Installation and adoption, not recommendations.
  • Teach the team to fish... we build capability, not dependency.
  • AI as leverage, not hype.
How We Deliver

Extract → Blueprint → Install

Extract

Facilitated working sessions with the founder, CEO, and leadership team... plus, where the work demands it, the operators closest to the process. We surface constraints, revenue leaks, decision rights, and the real work of the business. We do not arrive with answers. We extract what your team already knows but has not yet organized.

Blueprint

The truth, converted into a written operating Blueprint... your Annual Plan and the 30/60/90-day execution plans that deliver it: what to do, in what order, how success is measured, and who is accountable. Upside quantified in low, base, and aggressive scenarios. A document you own and execute from... not a deck, not notes.

Install

The system runs Monday morning: templates, automation, meeting cadence, scoreboards, and team training for adoption. Standard workflows come from TPG’s catalog of proven workflows, installed by our own engineering team. Custom needs are scoped independently. Your team leaves with AI fluency... installed tools they use daily, not a training session.
