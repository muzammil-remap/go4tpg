Questions, answered

How long is the summit?

One full working day with your leadership and execution team. The exact schedule is set with you when we scope the session. The two 90-minute installation sessions follow within ten days, and the written Strategic Blueprint arrives within 24 hours of the summit itself.

Who should be in the room?

The CEO or founder, plus the leadership and execution team... typically 3 to 6 people. We need the people who run the business day-to-day, not just the strategy seat.

What do I receive?

Two written deliverables. The Strategic Blueprint: priorities, metrics, accountability, the 90-day plan, the scoreboard, and a clear recommendation. The AI Adoption Blueprint: the opportunities we surfaced and the top 2 to 3 standard workflows, installed hands-on with your team.

What is standard and what is custom?

The workflows we install come from TPG’s catalog of proven workflows... they run on mainstream platforms with no custom code. Custom development and integrations are always scoped independently, in writing, before we build.

What if we want to move to a Sprint?

Your summit investment is credited in full toward a Sprint booked within 14 days. Most clients decide within the two installation sessions... by then the upside is quantified.
